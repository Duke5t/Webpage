About

Spencer Brule

With a soulful blend of expertise and passion, Spencer Brule stands as a pillar in the realm of saxophone education. Renowned for his commitment to excellence and unwavering dedication to mentorship, Spencer has carved a distinguished path as a professional saxophonist and teacher.

After completing a Bachelor’s of Music Degree at the University of Toronto, Spencer embarked on a mission to inspire and educate aspiring saxophonists. His teaching methodology is a harmonious blend of tradition and innovation, rooted in classical techniques while embracing contemporary trends. Spencer believes in a dynamic approach to teaching which fosters creativity and self-expression, nurturing the passion and inspiring the confidence of saxophonists.

Having held esteemed positions such as Lead Saxophone Instructor at Canadian Forces Music Division, Leader of CAF Saxophone Quartet, and Saxophone Section Leader in RCAF Band, Spencer has inspired countless colleagues and students over his 10+ years of experience. Spencer Brule is a gifted saxophonist having performed internationally at the North American Saxophone Alliance 2022 in Hattiesburg, MS, and at the Canadian Embassy in Washington, DC. Spencer has also performed for heads of state such as the Queen of England in London, UK, Prime Minister Justin Trudeau, and Governor General Julie Payette in Ottawa, ON.

His contributions to the world of Saxophone extend far and wide, through masterclasses, clinics, private lessons, and performances leaving an indelible mark on the next generation of saxophonists.

Spencer Brule resides in Winnipeg, MB where he spends his days as a professional saxophonist and educator.
